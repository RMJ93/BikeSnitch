Our Goal

Bike theft has been a large public crime, especially on UC campuses. For our project we will be designing a bike lock that integrates Bluetooth connectivity, GPS tracking, and a renewable power source. Our projectâ€™s goal is to reduce the crime rate of stolen bicycles by preventing and locating them.
